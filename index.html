Welcome to IND E 321 TA Session

TA session is a R and Minitab bootcamp. Here you can find all the materials (slides and codes) used for IND E 321 TA session in spring 2015 at Industrial Engneering department at University of Washington.

The object of this bootcamp is not only to introduce the basic operations of statistical programming softwares, but also to help you do statistical quality control with R and Minitab. Thus the TA session intends to include the introduction of R, quality control & hypothesis testing in R and Minitab, and also the homework solution sessions.

For the latest slides and developments on bootcamp materials, check out my github repository.

Bootcamp Modules

Each module of the R and Minitab bootcamp is independent and takes about 80 mininutes in scheduled friday morning, from 9:00 am to 10:20 am, in this spring quarter. Hence please bring your laptop with R and RStudio preinstalled before friday's lecture.
